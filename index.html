About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Accounting and an MBA from Zimbabwe Open University, which provide me with a unique analytical perspective on software projects.

Currently, I am enhancing my skills as a software development student at BYU-Idaho, focusing on dynamic web technologies, responsive design, and creating interactive web experiences.

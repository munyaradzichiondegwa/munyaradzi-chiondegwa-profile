About Me

My name is Munyaradzi Chiondegwa, and I am from Harare, Zimbabwe. As a member of the Deaf Community, I am passionate about leveraging technology to bridge communication gaps. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Accounting and an MBA from Zimbabwe Open University, which provide me with a unique analytical perspective on software projects.

Currently, I am enhancing my skills as a software development student at BYU-Idaho, focusing on dynamic web technologies, responsive design, and creating interactive web experiences. My ultimate goal is to collaborate with other developers on software that can convert sign language to written or spoken language and vice versa, making a significant contribution to accessibility and inclusivity.
